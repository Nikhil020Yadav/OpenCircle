INTRODUCTION


Mental health problems are conditions that affect your mood, thinking and behavior. They can be severe and can interfere with your daily life.



Just like physical health, mental health is important at every stage of life, from childhood and adolescence through adulthood.
There are many different types of mental health problems. Some of the most common include:
Anxiety disorders Depression Bipolar disorder Eating disorders Post-traumatic stress disorder (PTSD) Obsessive-compulsive disorder (OCD) Schizophrenia
